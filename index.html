Crystal Collector

You wil be given a random number at the start of the game to match.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

To win the game match your total score to the "number to match." You lose the game if the total score goes above that number.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal and the number to match.
